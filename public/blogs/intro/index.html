About me

Hi I am Zimeng! For non-Chinese speakers, you can just call me Michelle. Thank you for visiting my website. I will give you s short introduction about me:)

Born and raised in Chongqing China, I moved to Hong Kong for undergraduate study in Accounting and Finance, during which I had the chance to go to the University of Cambridge for a summer school program to study economics and literature and developed a genuine respect for the vibrancy and dynamics of the city of London. This brought me to my current school at London Business School, where I am reading for Masters in Financial Analysis.

I like to do data analysis and try to find the explanations behind the data. I am very glad and proud to present in this website the projects that I have been working on in 1o weeks of journey of learning R.
